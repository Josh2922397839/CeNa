About my Business

CeNa Pastry, is a small business founded by Ellianna Johnson and Eliana Lightbourne, designed to bring happiness and comfort to customers and was made with affordability for the desserts and pastries in mind.

We strive for flexibility for those with allergies and lactose intolerance. We accept all with open arms and we also have a website for customers to go on for online orders if they are introverted or might not be at the location as yet.

This website also contains a form for the customers in case they have severe allergies and must stay away from certain foods as far as possible. This ensures the safety and wellbeing of our customers.

Executive Summary

CeNa Pastry is a small pastry and dessert business founded by Ellianna Johnson and Eliana Lightbourne. The business was created with the goal of bringing happiness, comfort, and affordability to customers through unique desserts, pastries, and drinks.

CeNa Pastry focuses on creativity, inclusivity, customer safety, and excellent customer service. Our business welcomes everyone with open arms, including customers with allergies, lactose intolerance, or dietary restrictions.

To ensure convenience and accessibility, CeNa Pastry also plans to provide an online ordering website for customers who may be introverted, unavailable to visit the location, or simply prefer online ordering. The website will also contain an allergy form so customers can notify us about severe allergies or ingredients they must avoid. This helps ensure the safety and wellbeing of every customer.

Our goal is to create a relaxing, comforting, and enjoyable experience through delicious pastries and thoughtful customer care.
