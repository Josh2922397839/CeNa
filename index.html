About my Business

CeNa Pastry, is a small business founded by Ellianna Johnson and Eliana Lightbourne, designed to bring happiness and comfort to customers and was made with affordability for the desserts and pastries in mind.

We strive for flexibility for those with allergies and lactose intolerance. We accept all with open arms and we also have a website for customers to go on for online orders if they are introverted or might not be at the location as yet.

This website also contains a form for the customers in case they have severe allergies and must stay away from certain foods as far as possible. This ensures the safety and wellbeing of our customers.

Executive Summary

CeNa Pastry is a small pastry and dessert business founded by Ellianna Johnson and Eliana Lightbourne. The business was created with the goal of bringing happiness, comfort, and affordability to customers through unique desserts, pastries, and drinks.

CeNa Pastry focuses on creativity, inclusivity, customer safety, and excellent customer service. Our business welcomes everyone with open arms, including customers with allergies, lactose intolerance, or dietary restrictions.

To ensure convenience and accessibility, CeNa Pastry also plans to provide an online ordering website for customers who may be introverted, unavailable to visit the location, or simply prefer online ordering. The website will also contain an allergy form so customers can notify us about severe allergies or ingredients they must avoid. This helps ensure the safety and wellbeing of every customer.

Our goal is to create a relaxing, comforting, and enjoyable experience through delicious pastries and thoughtful customer care.

Target Customers and Why

1. Students

Students are one of CeNa Pastry's main target customers because many students enjoy snacks, desserts, and drinks during lunch breaks, after school, or while socializing with friends. Affordable pricing and visually appealing desserts make the products attractive to young customers.

2. Teachers

Teachers are targeted customers because they often enjoy quick snacks, coffee-time desserts, and drinks during breaks or after long work hours. CeNa Pastry aims to provide comforting and relaxing treats that teachers can enjoy conveniently.

3. Families

Families are target customers because desserts and pastries are often shared during gatherings, celebrations, or relaxing moments together. CeNa Pastry offers products that are affordable and enjoyable for both children and adults.

4. Friends

Friends are important target customers because groups of friends frequently purchase snacks and drinks together while socializing, studying, or spending time together. The aesthetic and fun nature of CeNa Pastry's products encourages group purchases and sharing.

5. Neighbors

Neighbors are targeted because local community support is important for growing a small business. Nearby residents may purchase desserts and drinks regularly due to convenience and familiarity with the business.

6. Dessert Lovers

Dessert lovers are target customers because they actively seek sweet treats, creative pastries, and specialty drinks. CeNa Pastry's unique aesthetic desserts and creative menu items are designed to attract customers who enjoy trying new desserts.

7. Customers Seeking Affordable Treats

Many people want delicious desserts at reasonable prices. CeNa Pastry focuses on affordability so customers can enjoy quality pastries and drinks without spending too much money. This makes the business accessible to a wider range of customers.

Marketing Plan

Word of Mouth

CeNa Pastry plans to promote the business through word of mouth because satisfied customers are likely to recommend the business to their friends, family members, classmates, and coworkers. Positive recommendations help the business gain trust and attract more customers naturally.

Posters and Flyers

Posters and flyers will help advertise CeNa Pastry to people in schools, communities, and public areas. Bright and attractive posters can catch people's attention and help spread awareness about the business, products, prices, and promotions.

Social Media (With Parental Supervision)

Social media allows CeNa Pastry to reach a larger audience and showcase products through pictures and videos. Many customers enjoy seeing aesthetic desserts online before purchasing them. Parental supervision ensures safe and responsible use of social media platforms.

Attractive Packaging

Attractive packaging helps products look more professional, appealing, and memorable. Beautiful packaging can encourage customers to purchase products and share them online, which can increase business exposure and customer interest.

Loyalty Rewards

Loyalty rewards encourage customers to continue supporting CeNa Pastry regularly. Discounts, free treats, and membership benefits help customers feel appreciated and increase customer retention and satisfaction.

Samples and Promotions

Giving samples and running promotions allows customers to try products before purchasing them. This can attract new customers, increase interest in the business, and help people discover menu items they enjoy.

Online Marketing Through the Website

The CeNa Pastry website allows customers to view products, place orders, and learn more about the business online. Online marketing helps reach customers who may not be physically present, are introverted, or prefer ordering digitally for convenience.
